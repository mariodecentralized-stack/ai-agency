FAQ

Common questions.

Do I need to know anything about AI or tech?

Not at all. We handle 100% of the technical setup. You just describe your business and what you want to solve — we build everything. You'll get a plain-English walkthrough at the end.

How long does it take to go live?

Most projects go live within 7–14 business days from your discovery call. The Scale package takes up to 21 days. You'll receive regular progress updates throughout the build.

What if it doesn't work for my business?

We offer a 30-day results guarantee. If the system doesn't perform as promised after we've worked to fix it, we'll refund your full setup fee. No questions asked.

What tools do you use — and do I pay for them?

We use Make, n8n, Claude API, Voiceflow, Vapi, GoHighLevel, and others. Some have free tiers; most cost $20–$100/month. We give you the full cost breakdown before we start.

Can I add more automations later?

Absolutely — most clients start with one and add more after seeing results. Existing clients get discounted rates on all additional automations added to their retainer.

What types of businesses do you work with?

Clinics, gyms, restaurants, real estate agencies, coaches, marketing agencies, and e-commerce brands. If you have repetitive tasks and customer touchpoints, we can automate them.
